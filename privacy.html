Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026. Parasite Club USA (Alpharetta, GA, USA).

The short version

We collect the information needed to run the store, show you relevant frames, and improve our marketing. We use cookies and similar technologies — including analytics and advertising pixels — only after you accept them in our cookie banner. You can decline non-essential cookies at any time.

What we collect

  • You give us: email, account and order details, shipping address, and (for prescription lenses) the prescription information you provide.
  • Automatically: device/browser data, pages viewed, and how you found us (referrer and UTM/campaign tags).
  • Try-on camera: our virtual try-on uses your camera only while you’re on the page. Face-tracking happens on your device and is not recorded, stored, or sent to our servers.

Cookies & tracking technologies

With your consent, we use:

  • Analytics — Google Analytics 4, to understand site usage.
  • Advertising pixels — Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, and Google Ads, to measure campaigns and show relevant ads.
  • Essential — cookies required for the cart, checkout (Shop Pay), and security. These run regardless of consent.

We honor Google Consent Mode v2: ad and analytics storage stay off by default until you opt in.

Your choices

  • Accept or decline non-essential cookies in the banner.
  • Request access to or deletion of your data, or opt out of the “sale/sharing” of personal information (CCPA/CPRA), by emailing us.
  • Manage ad preferences directly with Meta, TikTok, and Google.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers that help us operate: Shopify (commerce & Shop Pay), our email platform (Klaviyo), analytics/ad platforms above, and — for prescription orders — our optical lab partner. We do not sell your personal information for money.

Questions or requests: edgarbluegroup@gmail.com.
